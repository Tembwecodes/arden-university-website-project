From cosmopolitan cities to pristine wilderness, discover South Africa's diverse landscapes

Premier Destinations

Cape Town - The Mother City

Where mountains meet the ocean

Cape Town captivates visitors with its stunning natural setting beneath Table Mountain, world-class wine regions, and vibrant cultural scene.

Must-Visit Attractions:

  • Table Mountain: Cable car rides with panoramic views
  • Robben Island: UNESCO World Heritage site
  • Cape Winelands: Stellenbosch and Franschhoek
  • Boulders Beach: African penguin colony

Johannesburg - City of Gold

Africa's financial capital

Johannesburg pulses with energy as South Africa's largest city and economic hub. Rich in history from the gold rush era.

Key Experiences:

  • Apartheid Museum: Comprehensive history
  • Soweto Township: Cultural tours
  • Constitution Hill: Former prison
  • Cradle of Humankind: Fossil sites
