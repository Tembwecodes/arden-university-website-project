About South Africa Tourism

Discover the story behind the Rainbow Nation and get expert support for your journey

The Rainbow Nation Welcomes You

South Africa, affectionately known as the Rainbow Nation, is a country of extraordinary diversity and natural beauty. From the dramatic coastlines of the Western and Eastern Cape to the vast savannas of the Lowveld, our landscapes are as varied as our people.

With 11 official languages and a rich tapestry of cultures, South Africa offers visitors an authentic African experience combined with world-class infrastructure and hospitality.

Our Tourism Heritage

South Africa's modern tourism industry began developing in the 1990s following democratic transformation. Today, we are proud to be one of Africa's leading tourism destinations, welcoming over 10 million international visitors annually.
